--encoding={sjis,utf16-le,utf16-be,utf8,detect}
	Defines the encoding used to decode the input, where:
	Value		Short arg	Meaning
	-----       ---------   -------
	sjis		-j			Shift JIS
	utf16-le	-l			UTF16 Little Endian
	utf16-Be	-b			UTF16 Big Endian
	utf8		-u			UTF8
	detect		-d			Detect the right encoding among the ones above

--strictness=N
	The the strictness of the conversion.
	The strictness is a value determining at what point a string is considered
	garbage and should not be displayed. The lower the value, the higher the
	chance of having garbage in the result. The higher the value, the higher
	the chance of mistakenly excluding a valid string.

--debug={i,g,7,v,V,6,d,D,n,f,e,s,r,x}, default: none
	Aimed at developers. Sets debugging flags, where:
	Flag	Information displayed
	----    ---------------------
	i		Hexadecimal versions of the converted lines
	g		Global hexadecimal inputs
	7		Validity mark of the the hexadecimal versions of the lines
	v		Validity mark of the strings
	V		7+v
	6		Details on the validity displayed by flag 7 (includes 7)
	d		Details on the validity displayed by flag v (includes v)
	D		6+d
	n		Non formatted version of the lines
	f		Lines rejected due to their validity
	e		Encoding detected for the converted lines (-d only)
	s		Validity mark of the encoding displayed by flag e (-d only)
	r		Details on the validity displayed by flag s (-d only, includes s)
	x		Lines converted with a rejected encoding (-d only)

--help (cannot be used with other options)
	Displays this message.

The default options are: --encoding=detect --strictness=20
